The product

First of all, this is a time bank implementation.

In a nutshell, a time bank is a reciprocity-based work trading system in which hours are the currency. With time banking, a person with one skill can exchange hours of work for equal hours of work in another skill.

In addition, we add communication and organization functions, which makes this concept easy, safe and fun to use.

Find a snap

Search for opportunities or create user profile and wait for it. Use the chat and comments. Choose tasks which makes sense.

Make some hours

Once you complete the task, you earn the time spent. Check your profile page for details. Ask the production team for help, if needed.

Ask for a help

With some hours in your wallet, you may ask for a help with something. Would you need help with your homework or with the garden?
